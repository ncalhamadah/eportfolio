In our class, we engaged in a collaborative discussion, examining the hurdles businesses encounter while transitioning into the digital era. This conversation highlighted the importance of proactive strategies before embarking on such transformations.

We found that every step towards automation carries inherent technological risks and threats. The research we present in the subsequent sections emphasizes these findings, demonstrating the vital relevance of this topic in our current module.

Tesla's reliance on vendor for batteries highlighted several key issues around planning, supplier dependencies, and the importance of managing expectations. The company's reliance on third-party suppliers for battery production resulted in significant delays when those suppliers were unable to meet Tesla's requirements. As Tesla relied heavily on its third-party battery supplier for its automation processes, the supplier's inability to deliver posed a significant risk to production. This disruption underlines the necessity of assessing vendor risks in the context of automation, including the capacity and reliability of third parties. Ensuring a backup plan or secondary vendors can mitigate these risks and ensure a smoother transition to automation.
‍Amazon's attempt to advance its operations with automation brought to light various concerns and complications. A notable incident involved an Amazon robot accidentally triggering a can of bear repellent, which resulted in multiple hospitalizations.
With discussing with peer reviews in our class, we have highlighted the transitioning to automation of Amazon could have leveraged proactive risk management techniques such as rigorous risk assessments and extensive testing. Risk assessments would identify potential safety or data breaches while thorough testing and conducting simulations to predict chances ensures any technical issues are addressed prior to full deployment, implementing an alert for unsual motions for example would help preventing incidents like the bear repellent accident.
